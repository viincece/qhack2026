# DG CNECT/2023/OP/0021 -- Media Market Monitoring

## Summary

A monitoring study of the European media market, commissioned by DG CNECT (Directorate-General for Communications Networks, Content and Technology). The project mapped 11,000+ media entities, analysed platform dependency patterns, assessed regulatory compliance, and delivered quarterly updates over 18 months.

## Deliverables

1. **Entity map**: 11,000+ media entities identified and profiled
2. **Platform dependency analysis**: Assessment of media organisations' dependencies on major digital platforms
3. **Regulatory compliance assessment**: Evaluation of compliance with relevant media regulations
4. **Quarterly updates**: Regular update cycles with change tracking
