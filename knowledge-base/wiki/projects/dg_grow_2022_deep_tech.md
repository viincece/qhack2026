# DG GROW/2022/OP/0011 -- Industrial Deep Tech Ecosystem

## Summary

A comprehensive mapping of Europe's industrial deep tech ecosystem across three sectors, commissioned by DG GROW (Directorate-General for Internal Market, Industry, Entrepreneurship and SMEs). The project produced datasets covering 9,800+ organisations, supply chain and IP analysis, longitudinal comparison, and a dashboard prototype.

## Deliverables

1. **3 sector datasets**: Covering 9,800+ organisations across three industrial deep tech sectors
2. **Supply chain / IP analysis**: Inter-organisational relationship mapping combined with intellectual property landscape analysis
3. **Longitudinal comparison**: Temporal analysis showing ecosystem evolution
4. **Dashboard prototype**: Interactive visualisation tool for exploring the ecosystem data
