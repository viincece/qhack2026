# BMWi/2021/FF/0047 -- Germany's AI Startup Landscape

## Summary

A mapping of Germany's AI startup ecosystem, commissioned by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy (BMWi). The project identified 1,400+ AI startups, analysed investor networks, and produced a policy brief on the state of Germany's AI startup landscape.

## Deliverables

1. **Startup database**: 1,400+ AI startups identified and profiled
2. **Investor network analysis**: Mapping of investment relationships in the German AI ecosystem
3. **Policy brief**: Summary findings and policy recommendations for BMWi
