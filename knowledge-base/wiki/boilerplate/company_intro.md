# Standard Company Introduction

## Short Version (1 paragraph)
Meridian Intelligence GmbH is a Berlin-based research and data analytics consultancy specialising in market intelligence for EU institutional clients and large-scale private sector organisations. Founded in 2016, Meridian employs approximately 35 staff — including 6 senior researchers, 8 data engineers, and 12 research analysts — and has completed more than 40 projects for the European Commission, EU regulatory agencies, German federal ministries, and leading research institutions. We combine proprietary web-based data infrastructure with rigorous research methodology to produce market intelligence that goes beyond what commercial databases can provide, particularly in niche and fragmented markets where standard data sources fall short.

## Extended Version (2 paragraphs)
Meridian Intelligence GmbH is a Berlin-based research and data analytics consultancy specialising in market intelligence for EU institutional clients and large-scale private sector organisations. Founded in 2016 and headquartered at Luisenstraße 52, 10117 Berlin, Meridian employs approximately 35 full-time equivalent staff — including 6 senior researchers, 8 data engineers, and 12 research analysts. Our annual turnover has grown steadily from EUR 3.1M (2022) to EUR 4.2M (2024), reflecting sustained demand for our services across EU institutions.

We help clients understand complex, fragmented markets — identifying who the relevant actors are, how they are distributed, how markets are evolving, and what the evidence base is for regulatory or strategic decisions. Our cumulative dataset footprint exceeds 80 million organisational web records processed across EU27 markets through more than 40 completed projects. Key clients include the European Commission (DG CNECT, DG GROW, JRC), EU regulatory agencies (ENISA, EBA), and German federal ministries.
