# Meridian Intelligence GmbH -- Company Profile

## Summary

Meridian Intelligence GmbH (MIG) is a Berlin-based market intelligence firm founded in 2016. The company specialises in evidence-based market and ecosystem mapping for EU institutional clients, with a particular focus on niche and fragmented markets where standard commercial databases fall short. MIG employs approximately 35 full-time equivalents and generated EUR 4.2 million in turnover in 2024.

## Core Positioning

MIG specialises in market intelligence for EU institutional clients, with a distinctive focus on:

- **Niche and fragmented markets** where standard databases (e.g., Orbis, Crunchbase) have poor coverage
- **Evidence-based identification** of entities that do not self-classify into policy-relevant categories
- **Web-scale data collection and NLP classification** via the proprietary [[methodology/webmap_pipeline]]
- **Policy-aligned taxonomies** that translate regulatory definitions into operational classification criteria
