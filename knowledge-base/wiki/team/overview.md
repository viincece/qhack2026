## Typical Project Staffing Patterns

Based on past projects (see [[projects/overview]]):

- **Large EU contract (EUR 400K+, 18-24mo)**: Becker (project director) + Vogel (data lead) + Chen (policy lead) + 1-2 junior staff. Example: [[projects/jrc_2024_data_economy]].
- **Medium EU contract (EUR 250-400K, 12-18mo)**: Becker (project director) + 1 senior lead + 1 junior. Example: [[projects/eba_2024_fintech_dora]], [[projects/dg_cnect_2023_media]].
- **Smaller/sub-contracts (under EUR 200K)**: 1 senior lead + junior support. Example: [[projects/bmwi_2021_ai_startups]], Fraunhofer ISI sub-contract.
